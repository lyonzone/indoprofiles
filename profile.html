INDO PROFILES

Our Company  incepted in 1995 under leadership of qualified & well experienced Person Mr.P.C.Das but  after joining by our dynamic,well qualified & result oriented person as Leader Mr.PRASANTA KUMAR DASsince 2007  we have not looked back. 

We started with small efforts but now we are leading manufacturer, importer, exporter ,supplier, stockiest and channel partner of all the industrial range products of Hydraulics,Pneumatics,Instrumentation & Automated ,Electrical Switchgear & industrials along with security systems in industries & offices.

We are leading & renowned manufacturer of Complete Electrical Pannel,LT transformer  as per customers specifications or drawings along with Hydraulic Power Pack with cylinders.


Initially we started at Rourkela, Orissa but soon we widened our arms and as consequence we have two offices located at ROURKELA and RASULGARH, BHUBANESWAR in ODISHA (India).
